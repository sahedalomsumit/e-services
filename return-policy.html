Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If something isn't right, our straightforward return process makes it easy to get a refund or exchange.

How It Works

Return Process in 4 Steps

1
Contact Us
Email us at sales@e-service.parts with your order number and reason for return within 14 days of receiving your order.
2
Get Authorisation
We'll review your request and issue a Return Merchandise Authorisation (RMA) number within 1–2 working days.
3
Ship It Back
Securely package the item with the RMA number clearly marked and send it to our Welham Green address.
4
Refund Processed
Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 working days.
Last Updated: 1 March 2026

Return Window

You have 14 calendar days from the date you receive your order to request a return. This is in accordance with the UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.

Important: Items must be returned in their original, unused condition with all packaging and accessories included.

Eligible Items

  • Spare parts and components in original, unopened packaging
  • Display units and touchscreens that have not been installed
  • Accessories and consumables in sealed packaging
  • Incorrect items sent due to our error (we cover return shipping)
  • Defective or damaged items (reported within 48 hours)

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom-configured or bespoke items made to your specifications
  • Software or digital products after download or activation
  • Items that have been installed, soldered, or modified
  • Consumables that have been opened or partially used
  • Clearance items marked as "non-returnable" at purchase

Refund Timeframes

  • Inspection: 1–3 working days after receipt
  • Refund processed: Within 5 working days of approval
  • Refund received: 3–5 additional working days depending on your payment provider
