Support & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most common questions about our parts, repair services, and trade accounts. Need more help? Contact our dedicated support team.

What industries does E-Service support?
E-Service provides spares, repairs, and after-sales services for the amusement, gaming, vending, and related machine-based industries.
What types of products can E-Service repair or service?
We support a wide range of components including bill validators, coin mechanisms, touchscreens, printers, hoppers, and many other electronic and mechanical parts.
Do you offer both on-site and workshop repair services?
Yes. We provide flexible service options, including professional in-house repairs and reliable on-site support depending on customer requirements.
Can E-Service supply replacement parts?
Absolutely. We source and supply genuine replacement parts from trusted manufacturers to ensure high performance and long-term reliability.
How quickly can repairs be completed?
Repair turnaround times vary depending on the component and its condition, but we aim to provide fast, efficient, and accurate service to minimise customer downtime.
Does E-Service offer technical support or troubleshooting?
Yes. Our technicians offer professional technical support, diagnostics, and advice to help operators resolve issues as quickly as possible.
Do you work with international manufacturers?
We maintain strong partnerships with major component manufacturers, enabling us to access the latest products, training, and technical resources.
Can customers set up an ongoing service or maintenance plan?
Yes. We offer flexible maintenance plans tailored to operational needs, helping businesses keep machines running smoothly throughout the year.
How can I send a part for repair?
You can ship your components directly to our service centre. Once received, our team will assess, repair, and return your items promptly.
How do I contact E-Service for support or inquiries?
You can reach us through our customer service team by phone or email. We also provide a quick-response support option for urgent issues.
