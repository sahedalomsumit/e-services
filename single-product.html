High-brightness 23.4" IPS display kit, ideal for kiosk, gaming, and signage applications. Includes LCD panel, controller board, and cabling. Drop-in replacement compatible with major kiosk OEMs.

The 23.4" Display Kit is a versatile, high-performance IPS panel designed for integration into kiosks, gaming terminals, digital signage, and self-service environments. Featuring Full HD 1920×1080 resolution with wide viewing angles and high brightness (350 nits), this kit is a direct drop-in replacement for most standard 23-24" kiosk displays.
