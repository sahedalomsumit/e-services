Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyse site traffic. Here's everything you need to know about how we use them.

Last Updated: 1 March 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, keeping you logged in, and understanding how you use our site.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website, including the ability to add items to your cart or complete a purchase.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third-party services that we use:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Stripe/PayPal: Payment processing and fraud prevention
  • YouTube: If we embed product videos on product pages

These third parties have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.
