Who We Are

Built on Expertise,
Driven by Relationships

Our primary focus has always been on growing our professional service business to meet the needs of the operator, and this has been made possible by the excellent commercial relationships that we proudly enjoy with component manufacturers worldwide.

Our Journey

Two Decades of Growth

From a bold idea to the UK's largest independent operator — here's how E-Service grew.

2004
Founded — A First for the UK
E-Service is established by Mike Clokie as the UK's first dedicated and totally independent spares, service and after-sales support company for the amusement, gaming and vending sectors. The vision: deliver genuine quality service without manufacturer bias.
2007
Expanding the Parts Catalogue
Building on growing demand, E-Service broadens its parts inventory to cover bill validators, coin mechanisms, hoppers and a wide range of electronic components — forging direct relationships with global manufacturers.
2012
Workshop & On-Site Repair Launch
E-Service formally launches its professional repair division, offering both in-house workshop repairs and flexible on-site service. This marks the start of becoming a true full-service partner for operators across the UK.
2017
Eurocoin Display Partnership
E-Service enters a strategic partnership with Eurocoin Display Systems, adding digital signage, kiosk displays, and touchscreen technologies to the portfolio — extending reach into retail, transport, and self-service environments.
2021
Trade Account Programme Launched
E-Service introduces a formal trade account programme, offering volume pricing, priority support, and dedicated account management to professional operators and businesses across the UK and Ireland.
2024
20 Years — UK's Largest Independent
E-Service celebrates its 20th anniversary, having grown into the UK's largest independent spares and service provider for the sector. With 9+ product categories and coverage across the UK and Ireland, the company continues to expand and evolve.
