Privacy Policy

Learn how E-Service (Europe) Ltd collects, uses, stores and protects your personal data when you use our website and services.

Last Updated: 1 March 2026
Information We Collect How We Use It Cookies Data Sharing Data Retention Your Rights Contact Us

1. Information We Collect

E-Service (Europe) Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects personal information when you interact with our website at e-service.parts and when you use our services. The types of information we may collect include:

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and billing/shipping addresses when you create an account or place an order.
  • Order Information: Products purchased, order history, payment method details (processed securely by our payment providers), and delivery preferences.
  • Trade Account Applications: Business name, registered address, VAT number, trade references, and industry sector when applying for a trade account.
  • Communications: Information you provide when contacting us via email, phone, or our contact form, including enquiry details and correspondence records.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & Browser Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, referring URLs, and search queries used on our site.
  • Cookie Data: Information collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies (see our Cookie Policy for full details).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfil your orders, send order confirmations, dispatch notifications, and handle returns or exchanges.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your customer account, maintain your wishlist, and provide access to order history.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your enquiries, provide technical support, and resolve any issues with your orders or account.
  • Service Improvement: To analyse website usage patterns, improve our product catalogue, optimise the user experience, and develop new features.
  • Marketing Communications: To send you newsletters, special offers, and product updates — only with your explicit consent. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, including tax obligations and fraud prevention.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyse site traffic, and personalise content. For comprehensive information about the cookies we use and how to manage your preferences, please see our dedicated Cookie Policy.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with trusted third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: To securely process your payments (e.g., Stripe, PayPal). These providers have their own privacy policies and are PCI DSS compliant.
  • Delivery Partners: To fulfil and deliver your orders (e.g., Royal Mail, DPD, DHL). We share only the information necessary for delivery.
  • Analytics Services: To help us understand website usage and improve our services (e.g., Google Analytics). Data is anonymised where possible.
  • Legal Requirements: Where we are required to disclose information by law, court order, or governmental authority.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Customer Accounts: Data is retained for as long as your account remains active, plus 6 years after account closure for legal and accounting purposes.
  • Order Records: Retained for a minimum of 6 years to comply with UK tax and accounting regulations.
  • Marketing Consent: Retained until you withdraw your consent or unsubscribe.
  • Website Analytics: Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 26 months.

6. Your Rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on your consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 7 below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us:

  • Email: sales@e-service.parts
  • Post: E-Service (Europe) Ltd, Unit 4 Travellers Close, Welham Green, Hatfield, AL9 7JL, United Kingdom

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe your data protection rights have been violated. Visit ico.org.uk for more information.
